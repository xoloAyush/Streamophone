## Duolingo Max

## Purpose: Learn languages with AI.

It includes features beyond the regular Duolingo app.

Roleplay

You can have realistic conversations.

Example

You:

I'm at a restaurant in Spain.

AI:

¡Hola! ¿Qué desea comer?

You reply in Spanish, and the AI responds naturally.

Explain My Answer

If you make a mistake, instead of simply marking it wrong, it explains why.

Example:

You wrote:

Yo es estudiante.

AI explains:

"Es" is incorrect because "Yo" uses the verb form "soy." The correct sentence is:

Yo soy estudiante.
